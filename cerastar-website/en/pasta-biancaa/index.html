White body
Ceramic

for
WALLS

 

White ceramic tile is a type of traditionl ceramic material, very different from porcelain gres. It is used in the traditionally used in the production of tiles, dishes, vases, and other art objects. Characterized by a fine finish and both bright and muted colorations, white ceramic tile is appreciated for its elegance and versatility in wall covering.

This type of ceramic offers a smooth, lightweight, easy-to-work-with, and uniform surface that lends itself well to a variety of decorative coating applications. White ceramic tile can be glazed in different colors or decorated with artistic motifs to create unique and fascinating pieces. It easily adapts to different styles of décor, from classic to modern. It is often chosen to cover surfaces such as bathroom and kitchen walls and other residential and commercial surfaces, adding a touch of brightness and sophistication to the environments. It i salso available with refined and elegant 3D structures that turn every wall into pure beauty with palyfull light and dark effects.

Advantages
of white-body ceramic
for interior spaces

White-body ceramic wall coverings offer a number of extraordinary advantages thanks to their exceptional technical and production characteristics. They are the ideal solution for all types of interior walls in residential, commercial and public areas, guaranteeing a healthy environment that is easy to clean and maintain, as well as environmentally sustainable.

Ease of processing
and versatility
application

One of the main advantages of white-body wall tiles is their ease to work with. When compared to porcelain grers and natural stones, white body ceramic tiles allow greater application versatility, cuts, holes and trims with extreme ease, making possible the customisation of any wall.

HIGH YIELD
AESTHETICS

From an aesthetic point of view, white-body wall tiles offer a high aesthetic quality and allow for more pronounced three-dimensional effects. Their clean, bright and modern appearance is perfectly suited to a variety of design styles.

EASY CLEANING
AND DURABILITY

White body wall tiles coatings are extremely durable and require little maintenance. They are easy to clean with a simple cloth dampened with warm water and an everyday cleaning agent.

RESISTANCE TO UV RAYS
WATER, STAINS AND CHEMICALS

The firing of the colored pigments in ceramic glazes ensures UV resistance, preventing fading, dye off, or colour changes even when exposed to direct sunlight. The non-porous surface of white-body coatings also makes them optimal for environments subject to constant humidity, such as kitchens and bathrooms. Finally, white-body coatings are highly resistant to stains, chemicals and acids thanks to the protection of high-quality enamels.

DURABILITY
LONG TERM

White-body wall ceramic tile retains its original appearance over time without the need for special intervention. Investing in white-body ceramic tiles means investing in a long-term solution. Their strength and durability will ensure an impeccable appearance over time, reducing the need for frequent replacements or costly repairs.

HYGIENE
AND HEALTH

From a health point of view, white-body coatings are toxic-free and hypoallergenic, contributing to a safe and healthy indoor environment for all occupants. Thanks to their resistance to external agents, they also reduce the proliferation of bacteria, mould and other allergens, helping to maintain a healthy indoor environment.

SUSTAINABLE BEAUTY

White-body wall ceramic tiles are made from natural materials and are produced using processes that minimise their impact on the environment. Their long life and recyclability make them a sustainable choice for the ecologically conscious.


White-body ceramic tiles are therefore an excellent choice for those seeking a durable, aesthetically pleasing and easy-to-maintain material for their interior walls. With a combination of versatility, durability and health benefits, ceramic wall tiles stand out as one of the best options available on the market.
