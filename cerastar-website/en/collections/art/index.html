Wall tiles and floor tiles cement effect

From the meeting between tradition and avant-garde, ART tiles are born, a material inspired by cement, characterized by the naturalness of the surface, and by a refined range of neutral, soft and natural colours, enhanced by the large rectified formats. A contemporary, material and essential surface, made of porcelain stoneware with digital technology, available in four large rectified sizes plus decorated cement tiles, proposed in the small 20x20 size to enrich residential environments and commercial spaces with the contemporary taste of design and elegance.

The ART project is completed by the white body covering, made in 4 colours, some in common with the stoneware floor (tobacco and graphite) others with a slightly lighter shade (silk and pearl) available in the rectified 30.5x91.5 format and in two different three-dimensional structures, Net and Sign.

A project of surfaces with a strong character to cover floors and walls of residential and commercial environments with a contemporary feel: porcelain stoneware meets modern white-body coverings, enriched with three-dimensional material structures, and cement tiles, a modern interpretation of the surfaces created by manual skills of skilled craftsmen.

Unusual combinations of different materials, different colors and different finishes for contemporary and welcoming environments, in perfect balance between tradition and design.
